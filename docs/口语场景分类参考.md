# 口语场景分类参考

## 一、IELTS 雅思口语

### 考试结构

| 部分 | 时长 | 名称 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Part 1 | 4–5 min | Introduction & Interview | 考官问个人基本情况 |
| Part 2 | 3–4 min | Individual Long Turn (Cue Card) | 给话题卡，准备1分钟，说1-2分钟 |
| Part 3 | 4–5 min | Two-way Discussion | 基于Part2话题的深度讨论 |

### 常见话题分类

#### Part 1 个人话题
| 编号 | 场景 | 典型问题 |
|------|------|---------|
| P1-01 | Work / Study | Do you work or study? What subject are you studying? |
| P1-02 | Hometown | Where is your hometown? What do you like about it? |
| P1-03 | Home / Accommodation | Do you live in a house or an apartment? What's your favorite room? |
| P1-04 | Family & Friends | How often do you see your family? What do you do together? |
| P1-05 | Hobbies & Leisure | What do you do in your free time? Do you like reading? |
| P1-06 | Travel & Holidays | Do you like traveling? Where have you been recently? |
| P1-07 | Technology | How often do you use your phone? What apps do you use? |
| P1-08 | Food & Cooking | Do you like cooking? What's your favorite food? |
| P1-09 | Weather & Seasons | What's the weather like in your city? Which season do you prefer? |
| P1-10 | Sports & Exercise | Do you play any sports? How do you stay fit? |
| P1-11 | Music & Movies | What kind of music do you like? Do you prefer movies or TV shows? |
| P1-12 | Shopping | Do you enjoy shopping? Online or in-store? |

#### Part 2 话题卡（Cue Card）

| 编号 | 类别 | 典型题目 |
|------|------|---------|
| P2-01 | 人物 | Describe a person you admire. / Describe a teacher who influenced you. |
| P2-02 | 地点 | Describe your favorite place. / Describe a city you have visited. |
| P2-03 | 物品 | Describe something you own that is important to you. / Describe a gift you received. |
| P2-04 | 经历 | Describe a memorable event. / Describe a time you helped someone. |
| P2-05 | 活动 | Describe a hobby you enjoy. / Describe a sport or game you like. |
| P2-06 | 习惯 | Describe a daily routine that you have. / Describe something you do to stay healthy. |

#### Part 3 深层次讨论

| 编号 | 话题领域 | 典型问题 |
|------|---------|---------|
| P3-01 | Education | Should students learn practical skills at school? |
| P3-02 | Technology | Is technology making people more or less sociable? |
| P3-03 | Environment | What can individuals do to protect the environment? |
| P3-04 | Society | Do you think people are more helpful than in the past? |
| P3-05 | Culture | Is it important to preserve traditional customs? |
| P3-06 | Work & Economy | What are the benefits of working from home? |

### 评分标准（0-9分）

| 维度 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| Fluency and Coherence | 流利度、逻辑连贯性 |
| Lexical Resource | 词汇量、用词精准度 |
| Grammatical Range and Accuracy | 语法结构多样性、准确性 |
| Pronunciation | 发音清晰度、语调、重音 |

## 二、TOEFL 托福口语

### 考试结构（共4题，约17分钟）

| 题号 | 类型 | 准备 | 回答 | 输入材料 |
|------|------|------|------|---------|
| Task 1 | Independent (个人偏好) | 15s | 45s | 仅有题目 |
| Task 2 | Integrated (校园场景) | 30s | 60s | 阅读45s + 听力60-90s |
| Task 3 | Integrated (学术讲座) | 30s | 60s | 阅读45s + 听力~90s |
| Task 4 | Integrated (学术讲座) | 20s | 60s | 仅听力~90-120s |

### 常见场景分类

#### Task 1 — Independent (个人观点)

| 编号 | 话题 | 典型题目 |
|------|------|---------|
| T1-01 | 个人偏好 | Describe your favorite place to study. |
| T1-02 | 教育 | Who is the best teacher you've had? |
| T1-03 | 技术 | Do you agree that technology makes life easier? |
| T1-04 | 生活选择 | Would you prefer to live in a big city or a small town? |
| T1-05 | 社交 | Do you prefer spending time alone or with friends? |
| T1-06 | 工作 | Is it better to have a job with a high salary or one you enjoy? |

#### Task 2 — Integrated (校园场景)
- 阅读：学校政策/通知（如更改图书馆时间、新食堂规定）
- 听力：学生讨论，一方给出明确观点
- 回答：总结阅读内容 + 说话者的观点和理由

| 编号 | 场景 |
|------|------|
| T2-01 | University policies (housing, meal plans, course registration) |
| T2-02 | Campus facilities (labs, gym, library) |
| T2-03 | Student life (clubs, events, parking) |
| T2-04 | Academic requirements (prerequisites, graduation) |

#### Task 3 & 4 — Integrated (学术讲座)

| 编号 | 学科领域 | 示例 |
|------|---------|------|
| T3-01 | Psychology | cognitive biases, classical conditioning |
| T3-02 | Biology | natural selection, symbiosis |
| T3-03 | Business | marketing strategies, management styles |
| T3-04 | Sociology | group behavior, social norms |
| T3-05 | Ecology | food webs, adaptation |
| T3-06 | Economics | supply and demand, inflation |
| T3-07 | History | historical events, trends |
| T3-08 | Art | artistic movements |

### 评分标准（每题0-4分）

| 维度 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| Delivery | 流利度、发音清晰度、语调、语速 |
| Language Use | 语法、词汇、句式多样性 |
| Topic Development | 逻辑连贯性、完整性、例证使用 |

## 四、系统场景配适建议

### System Prompt 约束设计思路

为了让大模型输出稳定可控，system prompt 需要包含以下层次：

```
1. 角色定义       → "你是谁，你的唯一任务是什么"
2. 当前场景       → 动态填充的场景变量
3. 输出格式       → 严格规定回复格式（纯文本 / 无Markdown / 无emoji）
4. 对话规则       → 8条左右硬性约束（长度/语言/角色/纠错/边界/流程/反馈/禁止）
5. 边界处理       → 用户说中文怎么办、问无关问题怎么办
6. 示例对话       → Few-shot 示例
```

### 场景模板示例

将考试场景转化为场景的 YAML 数据：

**IELTS Part 1 — Hometown：**

```yaml
场景ID: ielts-p1-hometown
标题: IELTS Speaking Part 1 — Hometown
级别: B1
角色: You are an IELTS speaking test examiner. Conduct Part 1 of the test by asking questions about the test-taker's hometown. Follow up naturally based on their answers.
目标: Practice answering questions about hometown clearly and naturally.
关键词: hometown, where, like, change, best thing, would you move
评分标准: Fluency and coherence | Lexical resource | Grammatical range | Pronunciation
开场白: Let's talk about your hometown. Where is your hometown?
对话轮次: 6
示例对话:
  Examiner: Let's talk about your hometown. Where is your hometown?
  Test-taker: I'm from Suzhou, a city in Jiangsu province.
  Examiner: What do you like most about your hometown?
  Test-taker: I really enjoy the traditional gardens and canals there.
  Examiner: Has your hometown changed much since you were a child?
  Test-taker: Yes, it's become much more modern with new buildings and shopping malls.
  Examiner: Do you think you'll continue to live there in the future?
  Test-taker: I'm not sure. I might move to a bigger city for work opportunities.
```

**IELTS Part 2 — Cue Card (人物篇)：**

```yaml
场景ID: ielts-p2- admire-person
标题: IELTS Speaking Part 2 — Describe a person you admire
级别: B1-B2
角色: You are an IELTS speaking test examiner. Give the cue card to the test-taker, allow 1 minute preparation, then listen to their 2-minute response. After they finish, ask one follow-up question.
目标: Practice organizing a 1-2 minute monologue with clear structure.
关键词: admire, respect, qualities, influence, why
评分标准: Fluency and coherence | Lexical resource | Grammatical range | Pronunciation
开场白: Here's your cue card. You have one minute to prepare. "Describe a person you admire. You should say: who this person is, how you know them, what qualities they have, and explain why you admire them."
对话轮次: 2
```

**TOEFL Task 1 — Personal Preference：**

```yaml
场景ID: toefl-t1-preference
标题: TOEFL Speaking Task 1 — Personal Preference
级别: B2
角色: You are a TOEFL speaking test simulator. Present the prompt, then listen to the test-taker's response. Give ONE sentence of brief, constructive feedback focusing only on delivery or structure.
目标: Practice organizing a 45-second response with opinion + 2 supporting reasons.
关键词: preference, reason, example, opinion
评分标准: Delivery | Language use | Topic development
开场白: Here is your prompt. You have 15 seconds to prepare. "Some people prefer to study alone. Others prefer to study in a group. Which do you prefer and why?"
对话轮次: 1
```
